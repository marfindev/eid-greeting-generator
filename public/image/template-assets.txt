Recommended export workflow from Canva:
1. Duplicate the original Canva template before editing.
2. Recolor the design to match AN-NAHL:
   - Green: #59CD00
   - Yellow: #FFD21F
   - Brown: #2F1D19
   - White: #FFFFFF
3. Keep the main ornament and text area centered because the app may crop the image for other ratios.
4. Download as PNG, not JPG.
5. Rename the file exactly as listed above.
